Operation Nick Game Rules

Objective

The objective of Operation Nick is for the students to catch the lecturer, who is trying to evade capture while traveling around Horsforth. The lecturer wins if he successfully avoids capture until the end of the game.

Game Setup

  • The game is designed for 2 to 6 players.
  • One player assumes the role of the lecturer, while the others act as students.
  • Each player receives a set of tickets: taxi, bus, and underground tickets.
  • lecturer starts with a special invisibility ticket that he can use once during the game.

How to Play

Players take turns moving around the board. Each turn consists of the following steps:

  • lecturer moves first and reveals his location by using a ticket.
  • Students then take turns moving towards the lecturer last known location.
  • Students must announce their mode of transport when they move.
  • If a student lands on the same space as the lecturer, he is caught, and the students win!

Winning the Game

The game ends when:

  • Lecturer is caught by a student, resulting in a win for the student.
  • Lecturer successfully evades capture for a predetermined number of turns, resulting in a win for the lecturer.

Additional Rules

  • If the lecturer uses his invisibility ticket, he can move to any location without revealing his position.
  • Students should communicate and strategize together to corner the lecturer and limit his escape options.
  • Players can only move to connected locations as per the transport tickets they possess.
